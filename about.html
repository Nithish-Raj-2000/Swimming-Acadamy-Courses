How It Began

From One Coach to an Academy

In 2014, former national swimmer Elena Cruz started teaching twelve students at a community pool every weekend. Parents noticed something unusual — nervous beginners turned into confident swimmers, fast.

Word spread quickly. Within three years, Stackly had its first heated indoor facility, six full-time trainers, and a curriculum built from real competitive coaching experience — not just theory.

Elena CruzFounder & Head of Coaching
What We Stand For

Mission & Values

Mission

To make every person — regardless of age, ability or fear — a safe, confident and joyful swimmer through expert coaching and personal attention.

Vision

To be the most trusted aquatic academy in the region, setting the benchmark for safety, technique and student progress transparency.

Values

Safety first. Patience always. Progress tracked honestly. Every swimmer matters — from the 3-month-old baby to the 73-year-old first-timer.
