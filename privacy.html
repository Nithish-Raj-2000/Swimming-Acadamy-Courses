Last updated: June 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create a Stackly account, apply for admission, or contact us, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, age, emergency contact details and payment information. We also collect usage data such as login activity and class attendance to power your progress dashboard.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Operate your student, trainer or admin account and dashboard
  • Process admissions, payments and membership renewals
  • Track attendance, skill progress and issue certificates
  • Send class reminders, announcements and billing notices
  • Improve our programs, facilities and digital tools

3. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with trainers assigned to your program, payment processors for billing, and regulatory bodies where legally required.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard safeguards including encrypted storage and access controls to protect your data. No system is 100% secure, but we continuously review our practices to minimise risk.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting our support team. Parents/guardians may manage data on behalf of minors enrolled in our programs.

6. Children's Privacy

Programs for children under 13 require parent or guardian consent during admission. Parents can review, update or request deletion of their child's data at any time.

7. Contact Us

Questions about this policy can be sent to privacy@stackly.com or through our Contact page.
