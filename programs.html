Kids Learn-to-Swim

Small batches of 6, playful coaching, and a clear path from first floats to full strokes — built around each child's growing confidence.

  • Max 6 students per coach
  • Floating, breathing and stroke fundamentals
  • Weekly parent progress report
  • Certificate on completion

Adult Mastery

Judgment-free lessons for nervous beginners and stroke refinement for confident lap swimmers — at your own pace.

  • Personal trainer feedback each class
