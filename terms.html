2. Membership & Enrollment

Enrollment in any Stackly program is subject to availability and completion of the admission process, including any required guardian consent for minors. Membership plans renew automatically unless cancelled before the renewal date.

3. Payments & Refunds

Membership fees are billed monthly, quarterly or yearly depending on your selected plan. A one-time registration fee applies to new enrollments. Refunds for unused sessions are evaluated on a case-by-case basis — contact admissions within 7 days of the charge.

4. Code of Conduct

All students, parents and visitors are expected to follow pool safety rules and treat trainers and staff respectfully. Stackly reserves the right to suspend access for repeated violations.

5. Liability & Safety

Swimming carries inherent risk. While Stackly maintains certified lifeguards and rigorous safety standards, participants and guardians acknowledge this risk by enrolling in any program.

6. Cancellation Policy

Memberships may be cancelled anytime from your account dashboard. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing cycle — no partial-month refunds are issued.
